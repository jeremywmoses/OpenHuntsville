Huntsville's Professional Network

The Team

#openHSV was conceived as a free community resource for Huntsville's startup and small business population. Our goal was to create an open directory of independent professionals who provide their services to help fellow Huntsville-based businesses grow. Any professional or group representative is welcome to create a listing on #openHSV.

Behind every good idea, product, or community is a few starters. Behind #openHSV you will find ordinary people who had similar desires to change, to solve, to shape, to encourage, and to start now. Join us in our efforts to boost our local economy and help our business community achieve new heights.

We make, design, engineer, create, illuminate, record, capture, code, program, open, decide, speak, blend, paint, construct, launch, markup, ignite, form, defend, ink.
